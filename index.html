Ayrton Senna

The brief history of the brazilian pilot born on March 21, 1960

Here's a time line of Ayrton Senna life:

  • 1960 - March 21st is a special day in Brazilian history and international Motor Sports. On that date, in 1960, one of the biggest idols ever in motor racing was born in São Paulo: Ayrton Senna da Silva.
  • 1973 - His first official victory took place in the first official race in the kart in which he participated, in July 1973 at the Interlagos Karting Circuit.
  • 1974 - Jr kart category, São Paulo champion.
  • 1976 - Jr kart category, São Paulo champion.
  • 1977 - Won his first "South American Karting Championship"
  • 1978 - "Brazilian Kart Champion"
  • 1979 - "Brazilian Kart Champion"
  • 1980 - "South American Kart Championship"
  • 1981 - Started competing in Europe, winning the English Formula Ford 1600 championship (12 wins in 20 races).
  • 1982 - European and British Formula Ford 2000 champion (22 wins in 27 races).
  • 1983 - Won the English Formula 3 English Championship (thirteen wins in 21 races, 9 of them in a row).
  • 1983 - Ayrton Senna (first time in Formula 1)
  • 1984 - Formula 1 career: Toleman
  • 1985 - 1987 - Formula 1 career: Lotus
  • 1988 - 1993 - Formula 1 career: McLaren
  • 1994 - Formula 1 career: Williams
  • 1994 - Collision and the death.
